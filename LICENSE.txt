This software constitutes the AI Observers models (trained Matlab NNs) presented in the paper 

"Predicting Individual Quality Ratings of Compressed Images through Deep CNNs-based Artificial Observers"

by Lohic Fotio Tiotsop, Antonio Servetti, Marcus Barkowsky, Peter Pocta, Tomas Mizdos, Glenn Van Wallendael, Enrico Masala,

Signal Processing: Image Communications, vol. 112, Mar 2023, pp. 1-15, DOI: 10.1016/j.image.2022.116917 (ISSN: 0923-5965)  
Author's version: https://media.polito.it/web/res/2023/01/Fotio_SPIC2023_author_version.pdf
